Grill Master Chicken Wings

Description

This grilled wing recipe is always a hit! I grill them up before a party and keep them hot in a low oven. I always use Frank's RedHot sauce. It's got lots of flavor and isn't too spicy.

Ingredients

    Chicken wing

  • 3 pounds chicken wings, cut apart at joints, wing tips discard
  • ½ cup soy sauce
  • ½ cup Italian-style salad dressing

    • Sauce

  • ¼ cup butter
  • ¼ cup hot pepper sauce (such as Frank's RedHot®), or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce

Step

  1. Prepare wings: Combine chicken wings, soy sauce, and Italian dressing in a large resealable plastic bag; coat chicken wings with marinade, squeeze out excess air, and seal the bag. Marinate in the refrigerator, 4 hours to overnight.
  2. Preheat an outdoor grill for medium-high heat and lightly oil the grate.
  3. Prepare sauce: Melt butter in a small saucepan over low heat. Stir in hot pepper sauce and soy sauce until combined. Remove from heat and set aside.
  4. Remove chicken wings from marinade and pat dry; discard any remaining marinade. Cook on the preheated grill, turning occasionally, until chicken is browned and no longer pink, 25 to 30 minutes. An instant-read thermometer inserted near the bone should read 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).
  5. Place grilled wings in a large bowl. Pour butter sauce over wings; toss until well-coated.
